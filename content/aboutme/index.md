### About Me
I began learning to make video games in high school and quickly decided I wanted to try and make a career out of it, eventually attending Champlain College and completing a BS in Game Design with a minor in Game Programming in May of 2026. While I was a student at Champlain, I worked on a number of individual and cooperative projects with the advisement of industry veterans and feedback from some actively working within the industry.

Although I play most genres of games, I tend to find the most fun achievement hunting, especially in strategy and puzzle games. I 100%ed Civ 6 over 7 years, played Menace for 24 hours the over first three days it became available, and worked with a friend to get all achievements in Viscera Cleanup Detail.
